Sifa is a professional network on the [AT Protocol](https://atproto.com). Your profile is portable, your data sits on a Personal Data Server you can move, and your reputation is the work you've actually published. No score we made up.

This site covers the parts of Sifa that don't fit inside the app.

## Getting started

- [Create a Sifa account](/docs/create-account): sign in with an existing Atmosphere account, or set one up
- [Import from LinkedIn](/docs/import-linkedin): bring your positions, education, and skills across
- [Use your own domain as your handle](/docs/use-your-own-domain): swap `alice.bsky.social` for `alice.com`

## Atmosphere basics

If Sifa is your first Atmosphere app:

- [What is the Atmosphere?](/docs/what-is-the-atmosphere): the open network of apps that share one account
- [DIDs, handles, and providers](/docs/dids-handles-providers): the three parts of every Atmosphere account
- [Apps, AppViews, and the firehose](/docs/apps-appviews-firehose): how the same data shows up in multiple apps
- [Your data on a provider](/docs/your-data-on-a-provider): where Sifa records actually live, and how to look at them
- [Lexicons and integrations](/docs/lexicons-and-integrations): the open schemas that hold it all together

## Features

- [Activity feed](/docs/activity-feed): what shows up on your profile and why
- [Atom feeds](/docs/atom-feeds): subscribe to a Sifa profile from any feed reader, no account needed
- [External accounts](/docs/external-accounts): link your blog, GitHub, [ORCID](https://orcid.org), and other profiles
- [Standard.site cards](/docs/standard-site-cards): make your own site render as a rich card on Sifa, [Bluesky](https://bsky.app), and other AT Protocol apps

## Account management

- [Move your account to another provider](/docs/move-account): migrate without losing followers, posts, or Sifa records
- [Privacy and data](/docs/privacy-and-data): what's public, what isn't, what survives if you leave
