The activity feed on your Sifa profile is opinionated. We do not show everything you do on the AT Protocol. We show the things you made, not the things you reacted to.

The exact rules come from a taxonomy in the Sifa SDK. The tables below are generated from that taxonomy when this page is built, so they cannot drift out of sync with what the product actually does.

## What we leave out: "Did"

These are things you did on the network — likes, follows, reposts, RSVPs. We keep them off your public profile so the page stays a record of work, not a record of taps. The Bluesky app and the originating apps already show this side of you.

<ActivityRegistry tier="action" />

A third tier (`filtered`) covers configuration records, moderation records, and other infrastructure that we hide from every Sifa surface. Those records still live on your PDS; we just do not render them.

## Why it is set up this way

Most professional networks rank, score, or pad your profile with engagement metrics. We do not. Your profile is a record of work you did. If you want to know if someone is good at what they say they do, click through to the actual work.

## Want a different filter?

You cannot currently customise which categories show up on your profile. If there is a record type you think we should add — or remove — open an issue against [sifa-sdk](https://github.com/singi-labs/sifa-sdk) with a link to the lexicon and a short reason.
