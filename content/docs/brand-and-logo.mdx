Sifa ID is part of [Singi Labs](https://singi.dev). The logos, colours, and fonts for Sifa and its sibling projects live in one place: the [Singi Labs press kit](https://singi.dev/press/kit/).

## The marks

Sifa has two: a mark (the icon on its own) and a wordmark (the icon set next to "Sifa" in type). The press kit carries both, as SVG and PNG, on dark, light, and transparent backgrounds. Sifa's brand colour is `#4385BE`.

## The one rule

The files are MIT-licensed, so you can copy and redistribute them to link to Sifa, attribute it, or illustrate something you're writing. You just can't put the Sifa name or logo on a different product, or use them in a way that implies an affiliation or endorsement that isn't there. The files are free, but the names and logos stay trademarks.

Linking to a profile, embedding a card, or writing about Sifa is fine. Unsure? Ask: [guido@singi.dev](mailto:guido@singi.dev).

## Download

[singi.dev/press/kit](https://singi.dev/press/kit/) has every asset and the full guidelines, including the marks for the other Singi Labs projects.
